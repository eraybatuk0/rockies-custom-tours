About Rockies Custom Tours

Based in Calgary, we specialise in private luxury tours throughout the Canadian Rockies. Travel in our Black Chevrolet Tahoe with a professional driver-guide who knows the best photo stops and quiet viewpoints. We welcome couples, families and small groups — every itinerary is private and fully customizable.

  • Vehicle: Black Chevrolet Tahoe SUV
  • Capacity: Up to 6 guests
